FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes — completely free and open source. No account, no subscription, no hidden limits.

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing runs locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

How does the removal work?

It reverses the watermark's alpha blending mathematically to restore the exact original pixels, rather than repainting with AI.

Can I process many images at once?

Yes. Bulk parallel processing handles batches and lets you download them all as a ZIP.

Which watermark variants are supported?

The Gemini logo, the star overlay, and the Nano Banana variant are all covered.

Is there a CLI for developers?

Yes — a CLI and SDK are available to integrate watermark removal into your own pipelines.

Does it support videos?

Video removal is in beta and processes the overlay frame-by-frame, also fully in-browser.

What formats can I use?

JPG, PNG and WebP inputs are supported, with PNG or ZIP outputs.
